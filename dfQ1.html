Interconnected Sustainability Dimensions

e.g. Alternative fuel vehicles

It refers to vehicles powered by electricity, hydrogen, natural gas, or biofuels. The aim is to reduce dependence on gasoline or diesel and lower emissions. This transition offers eco-friendly transportation systems that promote community health by lowering pollution. From an economic perspective, it reduces fuel costs, and creates opportunities for further research and development. The adoption of these new technologies requires creating awareness for technology acceptance, which leads to a positive cultural shift towards sustainable living.
